About Us

Empowering Skills. Enabling Futures.

RKB Global Academy (Pvt) Ltd, established in 2023, is a registered limited liability company under the Registrar of Companies in Sri Lanka. As an accredited Vocational Training Institute under the Tertiary and Vocational Education Commission (TVEC) and approved by the Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment (SLBFE), RKB Global Academy stands as a trusted gateway to international career opportunities.

Located in Kalahedihena, Gampaha, the Academy specializes in providing high-quality vocational training programs designed to meet global job market demands. Through modern teaching methods, advanced technology, and innovative approaches, we equip individuals with practical skills, professional confidence, and international employability — shaping a new generation of skilled professionals ready to succeed worldwide.

Our Courses

Explore hands-on training programs designed for global career opportunities.

Welder

Develop the art of precision welding using MIG, TIG, and arc techniques. Learn how to interpret blueprints, join metal components, and follow safety standards for construction and manufacturing industries.

Grinder

Gain hands-on experience in metal grinding and finishing. This course covers equipment handling, surface preparation, and safety protocols to meet industrial fabrication requirements.

Steel Fabricator

Learn how to cut, shape, and assemble structural steel frameworks. This course focuses on blueprint reading, layout techniques, and the fabrication of steel components for global construction projects.

Heating & Gas Cutter

Master the techniques of oxy-acetylene cutting and heating. Students gain practical experience in metal cutting operations used in shipbuilding, construction, and manufacturing environments.

Electrician

Comprehensive training in electrical installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting. Learn to design and implement wiring systems for residential, commercial, and industrial setups.

Refrigeration & Airconditioning

Explore the principles of HVAC systems, including installation, repair, and servicing. This course combines theory with practical sessions to prepare students for global refrigeration and air conditioning careers.

Sand Blaster

Learn abrasive blasting techniques for cleaning, smoothing, and preparing surfaces before coating. Training includes industrial machinery operation and strict safety compliance.

Auto Painter

Get professional training in automotive refinishing. From surface preparation to spray-painting and polishing, this program equips you with the techniques needed in modern vehicle bodywork and restoration.

Scaffolder

Build expertise in constructing, securing, and dismantling scaffolding structures safely. The course emphasizes global safety standards, teamwork, and efficiency on construction sites.

House Keeping

Develop hospitality and facility maintenance skills, including cleaning procedures, guest service etiquette, and hygiene standards used in hotels and commercial properties worldwide.

Care Giver

Train to provide compassionate care for the elderly, disabled, and patients in medical or home settings. Learn essential caregiving techniques, communication, and international care standards.

Korean Language

Learn conversational and professional Korean with cultural immersion. This course prepares students for overseas employment opportunities in Korea by focusing on communication for daily and workplace use.

Japanese Language

Gain language proficiency and cultural awareness for employment or study in Japan. The program emphasizes speaking, listening, and reading skills aligned with Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) levels.

English Language

Strengthen your English for global communication. Focus on grammar, conversation, and writing for workplace and academic success through interactive sessions and modern teaching techniques.
